
# SIII — Shusha International Intellectuals Institute
## Academic Publishing Division

The **Shusha International Intellectuals Institute (SIII)** is an independent academic institution dedicated to advancing open, ethical, and internationally accessible scholarly communication.

Through its academic publishing division, SIII operates a centralized publisher portal providing unified access to a portfolio of **five peer‑reviewed academic journals**, each published on a **biannual basis**.

## Publishing Model

SIII journals operate under a unified publishing framework that emphasizes:

- Peer‑reviewed editorial processes  
- Biannual publication schedules  
- Open access dissemination  
- Editorial independence  
- Ethical publishing standards aligned with international best practices  

---

## Purpose of the Publisher Portal

This portal functions as a **publisher‑level gateway**, connecting authors, editors, reviewers, and readers to SIII’s journal portfolio while preserving the operational independence of each journal.
